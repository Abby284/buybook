Destiny

by Abhishek Prakash

"We meet for a reason."

Destiny is a raw and emotionally gripping romcom novel (with a touch of the cinematic, for fans of Jab We Met and the Before trilogy). It tells the story of two strangers who meet on a random night in Delhi. Both Arjun and Neha are broken souls, unaware that their unexpected, unplanned (and somewhat kidnapped) journey together will ultimately make them whole. Follow their story—from strangers to best friends, from healers to lovers, only to potentially become strangers again, or perhaps remain together forever, if only in their minds, as they always imagined.

With heartfelt storytelling and a dose of chaos, Destiny explores trauma, self-discovery, attachment, and the blurry line between delusion and reality. Not every story ends in love — but some remain unforgettable.
