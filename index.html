Destiny

by Abhishek Prakash

"We meet for a reason."

Destiny is a raw and emotionally gripping romcom novel (and a little filmy story for Jab We Met and Before trilogy fans) of two strangers who meet each other on a random night in Delhi. Both of their souls were broken, not knowing the unexpected, unplanned (kidnapped) journey with each other will make them whole again. Follow the story of Arjun and Neha — from strangers to best friends, from healers to lovers, only to end up as strangers again or maybe together forever in their heads as they always thought to end.

Heartfelt storytelling, and a blend of hope and heartbreak, this novel explores trauma, self-healing, and the mystery of what's real and what's not — and how not every delulu is solulu.
